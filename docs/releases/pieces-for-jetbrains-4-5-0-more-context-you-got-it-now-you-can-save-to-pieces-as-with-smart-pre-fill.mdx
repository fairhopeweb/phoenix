# Pieces for JetBrains 4.5.0: More Context? You got it ✅ Now you can “Save to Pieces As…” with Smart Pre-Fill 🤯
> Mon Mar 27 2023 17:39:34 GMT+0000 (Coordinated Universal Time)
<p>The most valuable features of Pieces are quickly saving snippets and effortlessly enriching them. Today, we’re introducing an additional method to save and enrich your snippets in JetBrains: Save to Pieces As.</p><p>Just like the “Save As” you know and love, when you select this option, a dialogue window will pop up with pre-generated context, e.g., a title, tags, a description, related links, related people and more. From that window, you can edit all of the contextual metadata of your snippet.</p><p>You’ve always been able to edit this metadata, but now you can do so without additional clicks or navigating to a different application.</p><p>To use “Save to Pieces As...”:</p><ul><li>Highlight a chunk of code in your IDE</li><li>Right-click</li><li>Select “Save to Pieces As...” from the context menu</li></ul><figure class="w-richtext-figure-type-image w-richtext-align-center" data-rt-type="image" data-rt-align="center"><div><img alt="The right-click context menu for Pieces for JetBrains." src="https://uploads-ssl.webflow.com/6143afec68f555387049efb3/635165301972308229cb03b1_ScreenShot2022-10-19at11_04_16AM_55d2121a2e60f56dee2ce2206672882c_800.png" /></div></figure><ul><li>This pre-filled window will pop up:</li></ul><figure class="w-richtext-figure-type-image w-richtext-align-center" data-rt-type="image" data-rt-align="center"><div><img alt="The Save to Pieces As... menu." src="https://uploads-ssl.webflow.com/6143afec68f555387049efb3/6351653019723052cfcb03b0_image_1f5829b7a2d3106b148ebf4b48e3a6c5_800.png" /></div></figure><ul><li>In this dialogue, you can modify the automatically generated metadata and add your own.</li><li>Once you’re done, hit “OK” and your snippet - and its context - will be instantly saved to Pieces.</li></ul><h2 id="71eip">Join our Beta Testing Program</h2><p>Do you want to try the newest features of Pieces before they’re released?
